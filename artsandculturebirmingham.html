Volunteer within Birmingham's arts scene!

Midlands Arts Centre

The Midlands Arts Centre (MAC) aim to promote innovative, creative arts activities in ways which help to establish them as an important part of people's lives. MAC's volunteer scheme is designed to help support their busy programme of arts events and activities. Volunteer responsibilities include welcoming and supporting visitors, being a runner for events, providing information to visitors, supporting external events, marketing tasks like leaflet distribution, and gallery invigilation.
