BBC Wildlife Trust: Birmingham and the Black Country

This branch of the BBC Wildlife Trust is dedicated to demonstrating nature's essential contribution to the health, prosperity and wellbeing of everyone who lives and works in Birmingham. At this trust, volunteers look after amazing wildlife sites, undertake wildlife surveys in the field, and monitor species populations. There are also office placements available for volunteers with specific skills.
